# Design Notes

These notes capture the early thinking behind *A Quiet Tree*.  
They are not final – they are seeds, ideas, and fragments that may grow or fall away.

## Themes

- stillness  
- gentle growth  
- quiet companionship  
- noticing small changes  
- the emotional weight of slow time  

## Early Mechanics (Exploratory)

- subtle interactions with the tree  
- small environmental shifts  
- soft audio cues  
- visual changes tied to time or presence  

Nothing here is fixed. The design will emerge slowly, shaped by what feels true to the project’s tone.
