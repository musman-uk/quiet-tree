# Overview

*A Quiet Tree* is a small interactive piece about presence, patience, and quiet growth.  
The player is invited to slow down, to notice subtle changes, and to engage with a world that does not demand anything from them.

The game centres on a single tree – not as a mechanic, but as a companion.  
It grows slowly. It responds gently. It exists without urgency.

This document outlines the early conceptual direction of the project.
